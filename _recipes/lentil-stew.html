Ingredients

  • 500 grams of lentils
  • 2-3 onions
  • 2 tomatoes
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 1 hot chilli
  • oil
  • salt, pepper

Method of preparation

Heat the oil and add the onions and the carrot. Add the lentils, tomatoes, tomato paste, chilli, salt and pepper.
