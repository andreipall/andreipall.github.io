Ingredients:

  • coffee, two tablespoons for every 150 ml
  • heat source to boil water
  • kettle
  • coffee mug or cup
  • sugar, two tea spoons
  • milk 25 ml

Method of preparation:

Place the kettle on your gas cooker and turn on the fire. When the water comes to a boil, add your coffee.

Remove the kettle from heat. Wait four to five minutes, once you see that all grounds have settled to the bottom of the kettle, pour the coffee into cups, add the sugar and the milk and youâ€™re ready to serve your coffee.
